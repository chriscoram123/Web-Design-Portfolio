WHAT WE CARE ABOUT

The Future of Work

Machine Intelligence is destroying millions of old jobs. New jobs will be created and the skill sets required for both old and new jobs will change in most industries and transform how and where people work.


Technology

Dr. Google is the gateway to all knowledge. Learners have millions of new tools at their fingertips. How will we navigate our “man enhanced by machine” versus “man against machine” futures?


Student Voice

Learning is more student-centered. How are modern curricula motivating students to take ownership of their own learning? How will we measure them holistically?


Teachers Matter

Teachers are the critical link to the development of the world's children. How are they using their professional knowledge and skills to mentor and support their students?


Innovation

Technology will replace jobs, but we're in a uniquely exciting time. A time in which we can create new startups and new employment opportunities. What kind of learning matters?


A Plan For Our Planet

193 countries signed up to support the UN's 17 sustainable development goals for our planet and its people. The plan included promises to end poverty, feed everyone, create stability and provide quality education and protect the future of our world.


Well Being

Beyond schoolwork, jobs, and income, how's your quality of life? Happiness, Health, Purpose, Security, and Civic Engagement matter.

Global Citizens

Communities are more diverse than ever before. Wanted: Active global citizens ready, willing, and able to embrace an interconnected world.


Equality

The strength and stability of nations and their economic health are driven by educational opportunity regardless of race, gender or economic status.


Privacy Watch

Big Data is being created, used and shared on a vast scale. There’s more opportunity but there are also more risks for cybersecurity and privacy protection.
